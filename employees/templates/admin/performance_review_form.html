Performance Review Guidelines
Rating Scale:
  • 5 Excellent - Exceeds expectations
  • 4 Good - Meets expectations well
  • 3 Average - Meets basic expectations
  • 2 Below Average - Needs improvement
  • 1 Poor - Significant improvement needed
Review Types:
  • Annual: Comprehensive yearly review
  • Quarterly: Regular quarterly assessment
  • Monthly: Monthly check-in
  • Probation: Probationary period review
Tips for Effective Reviews:
  • Be specific and provide examples
  • Focus on behaviors and outcomes
  • Set clear, achievable goals
  • Provide constructive feedback
  • Encourage employee input
