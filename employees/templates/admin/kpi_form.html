KPI Guidelines
KPI Best Practices:
  • Specific: Clearly defined and focused
  • Measurable: Quantifiable with numbers
  • Achievable: Realistic and attainable
  • Relevant: Aligned with business goals
  • Time-bound: Has a clear timeframe
Common KPI Examples:
  • Sales Revenue (₹)
  • Customer Satisfaction (%)
  • Project Completion Rate (%)
  • Response Time (hours)
  • Quality Score (%)
  • Training Hours (hours)
Weight Guidelines:
  • 1.0: Normal importance
  • 1.5: High importance
  • 2.0: Critical importance
  • 0.5: Lower importance
